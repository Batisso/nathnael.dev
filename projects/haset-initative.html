Overview

The Haset Initiative is a woman-led startup initiative based in Ethiopia, aimed at fostering entrepreneurship among women across the country. The organization provides women with access to resources, mentorship, and financial support to build and scale their businesses. Through workshops, training programs, and networking opportunities, Haset Initiative is dedicated to breaking barriers and creating a thriving ecosystem for women entrepreneurs in Ethiopia.

As part of my front-end development portfolio, I was tasked with creating a responsive, user-friendly digital platform that reflects the initiative's mission and values. The goal was to build a website that not only shares the organization's story but also serves as a resource hub for women entrepreneurs across the country.

Problems

Limited Digital Presence
The Haset Initiative previously had no centralized digital platform to reach its intended audience. Potential members and partners had difficulty finding up-to-date information about the initiative’s programs and events.

User Accessibility
The target audience includes women from diverse educational backgrounds, many of whom are not highly tech-savvy. The website needed to be intuitive and easy to navigate for users who may not be familiar with navigating complex websites.

Lack of Engagement Tools
There was no easy way for women entrepreneurs to get involved, sign up for programs, or access resources. The lack of engagement tools made it difficult for the initiative to track interest and provide meaningful support.

Solutions

Responsive & Modern Website Design
I designed and developed a fully responsive website that adapts seamlessly to all screen sizes, from desktops to mobile phones. The clean and modern interface ensures a professional online presence that aligns with the mission of empowering women.

Simplified User Interface
I prioritized a user-centric design to ensure the website was easy to navigate. By implementing clear navigation menus, large call-to-action buttons, and concise content sections, even users with limited technical skills can easily find and access the information they need.

Resource Hub & Engagement Tools
I developed a dedicated section for resources designed to assist women entrepreneurs. Additionally, I incorporated an online registration system, allowing users to sign up for events, workshops, and mentorship programs directly through the site.

SEO Optimization
To help the Haset Initiative increase its visibility, I implemented SEO best practices, ensuring the site ranks higher on search engines for relevant keywords like "women entrepreneurship in Ethiopia" and "startup support for women."
